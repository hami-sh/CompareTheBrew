Who?

We are a small group of university students who were looking to get the most bang for our buck when buying drinks. After spending many hours calculating the price/standard drinks of alcoholic drinks by hand we wondered if there was a better and easier way to do this. So here it is!

Why?

To ensure that you, the consumer, are making the most efficient choice per your dollar. We know that it matters, especially those that are on a tight budget (looking at you university students)

How?

Drinks are returned by default with the greatest score first (drinks divided by price) and decreasing in order from there. We look at the websites for BWS (more to come soon) every evening at 12:00am to get the latest deals served just for you
